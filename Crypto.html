Course Description

The Cryptography course provides a comprehensive introduction to the principles and practices of cryptography. Students will learn about classical encryption techniques, symmetric and asymmetric key cryptography, digital signatures, hash functions, and cryptographic protocols. The course will also cover real-world applications of cryptography in securing data, networks, and communications.
