Course Description

The Network and Cyber Security course provides a comprehensive understanding of the principles and practices that ensure the safety of computer networks and data. Students will learn about network architecture, protocols, security mechanisms, cryptographic techniques, and ethical hacking. This course also covers how to identify vulnerabilities, protect network infrastructure, and respond to cyber threats effectively.
