Course Description

The Programming Fundamentals course introduces students to the basics of programming. It covers key concepts such as variables, data types, control structures, functions, and arrays. Students will learn how to write, compile, and debug code, as well as understand the principles of structured programming. The course provides a strong foundation for further studies in computer science and software development.
