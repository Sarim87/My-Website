Course Description

The Introduction to AI course provides an overview of the fundamental concepts and techniques used in artificial intelligence. Students will explore topics such as machine learning, neural networks, natural language processing, computer vision, and robotics. This course aims to equip students with the skills necessary to understand and apply AI algorithms to real-world problems.
